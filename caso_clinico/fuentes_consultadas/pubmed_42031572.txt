Título: Lumacaftor-Ivacaftor in Pediatric Patients With Cystic Fibrosis and Advanced Liver Disease: A Pilot Study.
Resumen: Lumacaftor-ivacaftor (LI) is a cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator (CFTR) modulator for patients with cystic fibrosis homozygous for the F508del-CFTR variant. All CFTR modulators, including LI, are metabolized in the liver, and their use in advanced cystic fibrosis-related liver disease (ACFRLD) is not recommended due to concerns of worsening liver impairment, despite potential benefits for nutrition and lung function.
